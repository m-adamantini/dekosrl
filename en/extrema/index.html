EXTREMA

The Extrema coating collection includes a wide range of spray coatings for internal and external applications for the cookware sector. The Extrema family of coatings for co-okware interiors consist of multilayered reinforced systems with a unique blend of parti-cles designed, tested, and approved in the Deko laboratories. These formulations provide significant advances in release properties compared to other fluoropolymer coatings, thus ensuring the best performance. Through the excel-lent abrasion resistance and outstanding wear-resistance characteristics, Extrema is the best solution for all types of cooking. Extrema has been designed on several different quality levels, aimed to optimize performance in accordance with the existing lines of application, representing an inno-vative system with very high performance.
