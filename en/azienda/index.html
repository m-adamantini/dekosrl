THE ORIGINS

A STORY OF TALENT

Ingenuity, know-how and curiosity define the character of Pierino Brunelli, founder and driving force behind Deko Srl. A chemical technician born in 1951 with a strong entrepreneurial spirit, he built over forty years of experience in the development of non-stick coatings for cookware. In 1994, with the invention of the sanding machine, he revolutionized the sanding process, almost completely eliminating pollution while doubling productivity. In 2004, he founded Deko. From that moment on, leveraging accumulated know-how and expertise shared with the team, the company rapidly established itself among the world’s leading players in non-stick cookware coatings and multiple industrial applications.

TIMELINE

LAMBDA
1983
The history of Deko begins in 1983, when Pierino Brunelli starts producing non-stick coatings.
1986
Pierino Brunelli founds Lambda, a company focused on innovation in materials and processes for the cookware coating sector. Lambda quickly becomes one of the world’s leading non-stick producers, reaching an annual output of 4,000 tons.
1999
Lambda is acquired by Akzo Nobel.
DEKO
2004
Pierino Brunelli establishes a new company: Deko Srl is founded in a small facility in the countryside near Piacenza.
2009
As business grows steadily, Deko Srl relocates to a larger facility of 1,700 sqm in Senna Lodigiana, where it is still based today.
2016
Continuous research and a skilled team of chemists allow Deko Srl to secure major Italian and international clients. Production space expands to 4,400 sqm, doubling laboratory areas dedicated to R&D.
2018
Equilybra Holding Italiana acquires control of Deko, strengthening its organizational structure.
2020
New investments are made in production, product portfolio development and a renewed brand strategy.
2021
Deko acquires major new clients in Central and South America.
2022
A new building is constructed, expanding the site to 10,000 sqm, with 5,200 sqm in use. Deko also obtains ISO 9001:2015 certification.
2023
Deko Brazil is established in Bento Gonçalves (RS).
2025
A new branch is established in Alexandria, Egypt.
